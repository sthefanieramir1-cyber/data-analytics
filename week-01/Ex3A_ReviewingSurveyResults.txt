9. Again, pretend you are the instructor. Do you think it was helpful or not helpful to make this survey anonymous? Why or why not?

I think it was helpful to make it anonymous. Students are more likely to be honest when they know the instructor cannot connect their answers to their name. For example someone might feel more comfortable saying they have no questions or admitting they are confused if there is no fear of being judged. The downside is that as the instructor I cannot follow up with specific students if I notice they need extra support. So it is a trade off but overall anonymity probably leads to more genuine responses which makes the data more useful.
